About Us

We are a student-led initiative that aims to empower students and academics to reduce risk from advanced AI, by:

🎯

Raising Awareness

Through discussion groups and speaker events

🛠️

Upskilling Members

In safety techniques through workshops and 1-1 support

🤝

Fostering Community

For high-quality discussion, support and connections to the wider field of AI safety

Our Impact

Our members have published in top AI and law conferences, participated in MATS and gone on to study AI ethics at Cambridge.

Whether you're a beginner curious about AI safety or an experienced researcher, you'll find a welcoming environment to learn, collaborate, and make a difference in ensuring AI development aligns with human values.
