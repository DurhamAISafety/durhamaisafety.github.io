About Us

Welcome to Durham's AI Safety Group! We are a student-led society dedicated to exploring and promoting the safe, beneficial, and ethical development of artificial intelligence.

Our mission is to foster a community of students and researchers passionate about understanding the long-term impacts of advanced AI, identifying potential risks, and contributing to solutions that ensure AI aligns with human values.

We host regular discussions, workshops, and speaker events, covering topics such as AI alignment, existential risk, AI ethics, and responsible innovation. Whether you're a beginner curious about AI safety or an experienced researcher, you'll find a welcoming environment to learn, collaborate, and make a difference.
