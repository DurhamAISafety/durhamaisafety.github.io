About Us

Our Mission

We are a student-led organization focused on the safe and beneficial development of artificial intelligence. Our group brings together students from diverse disciplines to learn about, discuss, and contribute to the field of AI safety.

Our activities include workshops, reading groups, guest lectures, and collaborative research projects aimed at developing a deeper understanding of the technical, ethical, and governance challenges related to advanced AI systems.

Why AI Safety?

As AI systems become more capable and widespread, ensuring their safe development becomes increasingly important. We believe that university students have a unique role to play in shaping the future of technology and addressing the challenges that come with powerful AI systems.
