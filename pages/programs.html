Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to advance AI safety research, education, and community engagement

Core Programs

Reading Groups

Weekly discussions of seminal AI safety papers, covering topics from alignment theory to interpretability research. Perfect for both beginners and advanced researchers.

Thursdays, 6:00 PM
All experience levels welcome

Workshop Series

Hands-on workshops covering practical AI safety techniques, from robustness testing to interpretability tools. Led by experts and graduate students.

Monthly workshops
Programming experience helpful

Research Projects

Collaborative research initiatives where students work on cutting-edge AI safety problems under faculty supervision, contributing to the global research community.

Open to all year groups
Publication opportunities

Speaker Series

Regular talks by leading AI safety researchers, industry experts, and policy makers, providing insights into the latest developments and career opportunities.

Industry leaders & academics
Q&A sessions included

Special Initiatives

Mentorship Program

Connecting students with experienced researchers and professionals in AI safety for guidance and career development.

AI Safety Challenge

Annual competition where teams develop innovative solutions to AI safety problems, with prizes and recognition for outstanding work.

Global Collaboration

Partnerships with other AI safety organizations worldwide, facilitating student exchanges and collaborative research projects.
