Our Programs

Intro Seminars

Overview

Our intro seminars are designed to make AI safety accessible to all backgrounds / studies. We cover key concepts for AI alignment and governance in a low-pressure environment, with opportunity for group discussion.

Each session is a presentation with significant breaks for questions and group discussion. We encourage 'dumb' questions!

Who's this for?

  • No technical background or prior knowledge required. Open to anyone interested in learning more about AI safety.

Recent Topics

Evals

Figuring out what models are capable of.

Mechanistic Interpretability

Figuring out what and how models think.

The Deepseek Revolution

Intro to AI's role in the US-China relationship.

In-Depth Reading Groups

Who's this for?

  • People from any background, preferably with some prior knowledge and understanding, who are interested in diving deep into the technical details of a niche field within AI safety research.

Overview

These provide an opportunity to engage deeply with seminal and/or recent AI safety work. In the past we've covered both technical research and AI governance/policy/law papers.

Anyone can suggest and briefly present a paper, before the group discusses it together.

Research Project Support

Overview

Interested in AI Safety research? We can support you to pursue it.

If you're interested, please reach out to chat with us (email in footer). Students: Please include your resume/CV and a brief description of your interest in AIS research.

Who's this for?

  • Faculty members interested in AI Safety who want signposting to funding opportunities and connections to researchers in the field.
  • Students who have past research experience, strong background in programming, or compelling demonstrated interest in AI safety.

Speaker Events

Who's this for?

  • People from any background who are keen to engage with experts in the field of AI safety and learn more about the latest developments.

Overview

These events offer a unique opportunity to explore emerging topics in the field, engage directly with speakers, and connect with others who share your interest in ensuring AI development benefits humanity.

To get involved and hear about upcoming speaker events, please join our discord server.

Social Events

Overview

Building community is our most important goal. Our social events provide relaxed, fun environments where both members and non-members can connect.

These events are open to all members and are a great way to meet new people who share your interest in AI safety while enjoying some downtime together.

Who's this for?

  • All members looking to connect with the community in a relaxed setting
  • Perfect for newcomers wanting to meet people
  • Anyone who enjoys deep conversations over food and games
