Our Programs

Intro Seminars

Overview

Our intro seminars are designed to make AI safety accessible to all backgrounds / studies. We cover key concepts for AI alignment and governance in a low-pressure environment, with opportunity for group discussion.

Each session is a presentation with significant breaks for questions and group discussion. We encourage 'dumb' questions!

Who's this for?

  • No technical background or prior knowledge required. Open to anyone interested in learning more about AI safety.

Recent Topics

Evals

Figuring out what models are capable of.

Mechanistic Interpretability

Figuring out what and how models think.

The Deepseek Revolution

Intro to AI's role in the US-China relationship.

In-Depth Reading Groups

Who's this for?

  • People from any background, preferably with some prior knowledge and understanding, who are interested in diving deep into the technical details of a niche field within AI safety research.

Overview

These provide an opportunity to engage deeply with seminal and/or recent AI safety work. In the past we've covered both technical research and AI governance/policy/law papers.

Anyone can suggest and briefly present a paper, before the group discusses it together.

Research Projects

Overview

Join impactful projects alongside Durham AISI researchers aimed at publication (eg. at a conference workshop).

This opportunity is offered to select members and by application. Feel free to reach out to aisdurham@googlegroups.com if you'd like to inquire about this opportunity. Please include your resume/CV and a short description of your interest in AI safety research.

Who's this for?

  • People who have past research experience, strong background in programming, or compelling demonstrated interest in AI safety.

    Social Events

    Overview

    Building community is at the heart of what we do. Our social events provide relaxed, fun environments where members can connect, unwind, and build lasting friendships beyond our academic activities.

    These events are open to all members and are a great way to meet new people who share your interest in AI safety while enjoying some downtime together.

    Who's this for?

    • All members looking to connect with the community in a relaxed setting
    • Perfect for newcomers wanting to meet people and feel welcomed
    • Anyone who enjoys good food, games, and great conversations
