Research

Advancing the frontiers of AI safety through collaborative research and innovation

Research Areas

AI Alignment

Ensuring AI systems pursue intended goals and remain beneficial as they become more capable.

Interpretability

Understanding how AI systems make decisions and what they learn from data.

Robustness

Building AI systems that perform reliably under diverse and challenging conditions.

AI Governance

Developing frameworks for responsible AI development and deployment.

Machine Learning Safety

Addressing safety concerns in current ML systems and training processes.

Empirical Research

Conducting experiments to test AI safety theories and validate proposed solutions.

Research Opportunities

Undergraduate Research

Structured research projects for undergraduates with faculty mentorship and opportunities for publication and conference presentation.

Graduate Collaboration

Opportunities for graduate students to lead research initiatives and collaborate with faculty on cutting-edge AI safety problems.

Industry Partnerships

Collaborative projects with AI companies and research institutes, providing real-world applications for AI safety research.

International Collaboration

Research exchanges and joint projects with AI safety groups at universities worldwide, expanding global research networks.
