What is AI Safety?

The Foundation

AI Safety is the field focused on ensuring that artificial intelligence systems are safe, beneficial, and aligned with human values as they become increasingly capable.

Safety

Ensuring AI systems operate reliably and don't cause unintended harm to humans or society.

Beneficial

Designing AI to actively promote human welfare and contribute positively to society.

Aligned

Ensuring AI systems understand and pursue goals that align with human values and intentions.

Key Challenges

The Alignment Problem

How do we ensure AI systems pursue the goals we actually want them to pursue, rather than just optimizing for what we tell them to optimize for?

Reward hacking and Goodhart's Law
Specification gaming and mesa-optimization

Interpretability

AI systems, especially deep neural networks, often function as "black boxes" where we can't understand how they make decisions.

Understanding model reasoning
Detecting and preventing bias

Capability Control

As AI systems become more capable, how do we maintain meaningful human oversight and control over their actions and decisions?

Human oversight mechanisms
Containment and monitoring

Robustness

Ensuring AI systems perform reliably across diverse conditions, including situations they weren't specifically trained for.

Adversarial attacks and defenses
Distribution shift and generalization

Why AI Safety Matters

Near-term Impacts

AI systems are already being deployed in critical areas like healthcare, transportation, and criminal justice. Ensuring these systems are safe and fair is crucial for preventing harm today.

Medium-term Considerations

As AI becomes more autonomous and capable, the potential for both positive and negative impacts grows. We need robust safety measures to maximize benefits while minimizing risks.

Long-term Futures

Advanced AI systems could fundamentally transform society. Working on AI safety now helps ensure that transformation is positive and aligned with human flourishing.

Global Coordination

AI safety requires international cooperation and coordination. Building a community of researchers and practitioners helps ensure safety considerations are integrated into AI development worldwide.
