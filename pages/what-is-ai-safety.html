What is AI Safety?

& Why It Matters

The Foundation

AI Safety is the field focused on ensuring that artificial intelligence systems are safe, beneficial, and aligned with human values as they become increasingly capable.

Safety

Ensuring AI systems operate reliably and don't cause unintended harm to humans or society.

Beneficial

Designing AI to actively promote human welfare and contribute positively to society.

Aligned

Ensuring AI systems understand and pursue goals that align with human values and intentions.

Why AI Safety Matters

1. AI systems far smarter than us may be created soon.

AI is advancing fast, and this progress may result in human-level AI. But human-level is not the limit, and shortly after, there's a good chance we'd see superintelligent AI.

Rapid advances in AI capabilities
Potential for explosive intelligence growth
Timeline uncertainty but potentially soon
Learn more about AI advancement

2. These systems may end up opposed to us.

AI systems may pursue their own goals, those goals may not match ours, and that may bring them into conflict with us.

The alignment problem: ensuring AI goals match human values
Interpretability: understanding how AI systems make decisions
Goal misalignment and unintended optimization
Learn more about AI goal pursuit

3. Consequences could be catastrophic, including human extinction.

AI may defeat us and take over, leading to humanity's extinction or permanent ruin. If we avoid this outcome, AI still has huge implications for the world, including great benefits if it's developed safely.

Catastrophic risk to humanity
Potential for irreversible global consequences
High stakes: enormous benefits if done right, catastrophe if done wrong
Learn more about AI conflict scenarios

4. We need to get our act together.

Experts are worried, but humanity doesn't have a real plan to avert disaster, and you may be able to help.

AI governance: developing policies and regulations
International coordination and cooperation
Technical research and safety solutions
Individual action and community building
